About Us

Army Navy Burger + Burrito is a proudly homegrown restaurant in the Philippines, known for serving bold, hearty, and freshly prepared comfort food. From juicy burgers to fully loaded burritos and signature Freedom Fries, every meal is made to satisfy big cravings.

Founded with the idea of bringing high-quality fast-casual dining to everyone, Army Navy focuses on fresh ingredients, made-to-order meals, and a simple but powerful mission: good food, served fast, without compromise.

Over the years, we’ve grown into one of the most recognizable casual dining brands in the country, loved by students, professionals, and late-night food lovers alike. Whether you’re grabbing a quick lunch or satisfying a midnight craving, Army Navy is always ready to serve.

Our menu is inspired by classic American comfort food with a modern twist — balancing flavor, freshness, and value. Every bite is designed to be filling, satisfying, and memorable.

More than just a restaurant, Army Navy is about community, comfort, and consistency. We continue to grow while staying true to what made us popular in the first place: simple food done right.
